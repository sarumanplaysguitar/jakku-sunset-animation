BB-8 on Jakku: The Stars Above

Backstory: After being separated from his best friend, the esteemed resistance pilot Poe Dameron, BB-8 must find a way to survive the night on Jakku, alone. As the sun sinks away beyond the endless dunes of sand, the droid must brave the dangers of the desert, with only the stars above to guide him.
